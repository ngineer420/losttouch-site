Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated July 2026

In short: losttou.ch is a participant, or intends to become a participant, in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites, at no additional cost to you. As an Amazon Associate, this site earns from qualifying purchases.

This page explains, in plain language, how that works and what it means for you as a reader.

What an affiliate link is

An affiliate link is a normal link to a product or service that includes a small tracking code identifying that the click came from this site. If you click that link and go on to make a qualifying purchase, the retailer (for example, Amazon) pays this site a small commission. You do not pay anything extra, and the price you see is the same price you'd see visiting the retailer directly.

Where you'll see them

Some articles on losttou.ch mention specific products — a keepsake box, a photo scanner, a fountain pen, a memory-keeping app — where that product is directly relevant to the topic. Any such mention that is (or may become) a paid affiliate link is marked with a dashed underline in the article text, and every article that contains these mentions carries a short reminder near the top of the page.

How we choose what to recommend

The rule on this site is simple: the content comes first. Products are chosen because they genuinely fit the guide they appear in, based on the author's own research and reasoning about what would actually help — not because a given retailer or brand pays a higher commission. Whether or not a link happens to carry a tracking tag at any given moment does not change which products are mentioned or how they're described.

A transparency note on where this site is in that process

losttou.ch is a new, independent project. At the time of writing, this site has applied for or is preparing to apply to affiliate programs including Amazon Associates, and not every link on the site is yet tagged with active tracking codes — some are plain links to the same pages, provided purely as a convenience while that process is underway. This disclosure page will always accurately reflect the site's actual participation status, and it will be kept up to date as that changes.

Your choices

You are never required to use any link on this site. Every product mentioned can be found independently by searching for it directly with any retailer of your choice. Using our links costs you nothing extra and is simply a way to support the free content on this site, if you'd like to.
